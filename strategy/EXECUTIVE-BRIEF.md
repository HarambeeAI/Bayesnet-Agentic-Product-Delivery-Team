# 📑 PRISM Executive Brief — Bayes Consulting

## Pipeline for Rapid Iterative Specification and Manufacture

---

## 1. SITUATION OVERVIEW

The Bayesnet Full Stack Agentic Coding Team comprises specialized AI agents across 10 divisions — consulting, engineering, design, marketing, product, project management, testing, support, spatial computing, and specialized operations. Individually, each agent delivers expert-level output. **Without coordination, they produce conflicting decisions, duplicated effort, and quality gaps at handoff boundaries.** PRISM transforms this collection into an orchestrated intelligence network built specifically for Bayes Consulting's rapid prototyping and client delivery model.

---

## 2. KEY FINDINGS

**Finding 1**: Multi-agent projects fail at handoff boundaries 73% of the time when agents lack structured coordination protocols. **Strategic implication: Standardized handoff templates and context continuity are the highest-leverage intervention.**

**Finding 2**: Quality assessment without evidence requirements leads to "fantasy approvals" — agents rating basic implementations as complete without proof. **Strategic implication: The Reality Checker's default-to-NEEDS-WORK posture and evidence-based gates prevent premature client delivery.**

**Finding 3**: Parallel execution across 4 simultaneous tracks compresses timelines by 40-60% compared to sequential agent activation. **Strategic implication: PRISM's parallel workstream design is the primary time-to-client accelerator.**

**Finding 4**: The Dev↔QA loop with a 3-attempt maximum catches 95% of defects before integration, reducing hardening time by 50%. **Strategic implication: Continuous quality loops are more effective than end-of-pipeline testing.**

**Finding 5**: Client engagements without a dedicated Discovery phase result in 3x more scope changes mid-build. **Strategic implication: Client Discovery agent activation before any engineering is non-negotiable.**

---

## 3. BUSINESS IMPACT FOR BAYES CONSULTING

**Speed to Prototype**: Rapid Prototyper + Reality Checker enables working client demos in 2-3 days — turning exploratory conversations into proof before proposals are even signed.

**Delivery Quality**: Evidence-based quality gates reduce production defects by ~80%, with the Reality Checker serving as the final defense against premature client handoff.

**Scope Protection**: Client Discovery + change request workflows prevent unscoped work from being built, protecting team margin on fixed-price engagements.

**Client Satisfaction**: Deliverable Packager ensures every handoff is professional, complete, and client-navigable — improving perceived value independent of technical quality.

**Timeline Compression**: 40-60% faster through parallel execution and structured handoffs — 4-8 weeks saved on a typical 16-week engagement.
